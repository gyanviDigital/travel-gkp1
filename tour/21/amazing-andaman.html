Amazing Andaman

Description

Destination: Port Blair - Ross Island - North Bay Island - Havelock Island - Mount Harriet - Port Blair

Located on the juncture of the Andaman Sea and the Bay of Bengal, Andaman and Nicobar Islands is a group of islands, known for the memorable experiences that they offer. Our well-planned Andaman package offers you the best of sights and sounds of this place. During the span of 6 nights and 7 days, explore gems like Port Blair, North Bay Island, Ross Island, Mount Harriet and Havelock Island. Andaman and Nicobar is an amazing mix of tranquility and thrilling adventure sports like snorkeling and scuba diving.

Highlights

  • Sightseeing in Port Blair including Anthropological Museum
  • Lights and Sound Show at Cellular Jail
  • Explore the picturesque Ross Island and North Bay Island
  • Snorkeling at Havelock Island
  • Leisure time at the beautiful Havelock Island
  • Day's excursion to the Elephant beach
  • Treat your eyes to the lovely views of the surroundings from Mount Harriet
